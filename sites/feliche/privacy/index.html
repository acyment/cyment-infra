Feliche Privacy Policy

Feliche is a birthday reminder and gift-tracking app designed to minimize how much of your personal data leaves your device.

What Feliche stores

Feliche stores birthday names, dates, reminder settings, and optional gift or greeting notes locally on your device so the app can schedule reminders.

What Feliche does not collect

  • No account is required.
  • No cloud backup or sync is required for core use.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No advertising identifiers are used for ad targeting.

Contacts access

If you choose to import birthdays from your contacts, Feliche reads contact birthday information so it can create entries in the app. Contact import is initiated by you and is not required to use the app.

Analytics

Feliche may use privacy-focused product analytics to understand high-level app usage and improve reliability and product decisions. These analytics are intended to avoid storing the contents of your birthday list.

Notifications

Feliche uses local notifications to remind you about birthdays, gifts, and greetings. Notification delivery relies on operating-system capabilities on your device.

Third-party services

Feliche may rely on infrastructure providers needed to distribute the app or support analytics and crash reporting. Those providers process data according to their own terms.

Contact

For privacy questions, email privacy@auguri.app.
